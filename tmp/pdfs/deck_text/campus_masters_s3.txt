A LEAGUE FOR EXTRAORDINARY GAMERS
Campus Masters is a regional competition that unites universities
across the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Jordan, Qatar, Bahrain, and Oman
through structured, high-quality esports tournaments.
Currently we have over 51 universities onboard and have successfully
completed 3 seasons with a combined prize pool of over 31,000 USD.
Our vision for this league is to serve both the univeristies and students
as a platform for discovering talent, building campus pride, and
fostering a thriving grassroots esports ecosystem.
We offer brands a reach to more than 20,000 students via on-campus
activations and digital content.
