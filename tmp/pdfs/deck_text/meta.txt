FARM
The Koko Krunch Cocoa Plant Plantation is a
mini-game set in a Nestlé Farm area. Players
begin by collecting a seed from the seed
house, then plant it in the soil, water it, and
grow a cocoa tree through interactive
actions. Once the tree fully grows, the player
is rewarded, turning the process of cocoa
cultivation into an engaging, educational
gameplay experience.
